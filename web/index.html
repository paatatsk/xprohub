HOW IT WORKS

Two sides. One transaction.

Every job has a customer who needs work done and a worker who does it. The platform connects them, holds the payment, and releases it when the work is confirmed.

CUSTOMER SIDE

1

Post a job

Describe what you need, set your budget, and publish. Workers in your area see it immediately.

2

Review and hire

Workers apply with their price and message. Pick the one you trust, and your payment is held securely.

3

Confirm and pay

When the work is done, confirm completion. The worker gets paid, and you can endorse a job well done.

WORKER SIDE

1

Find work

Browse the Live Market for jobs that match your skills, or publish your ID Card and let customers come to you.

2

Do the work, get paid

Your payment is secured before you start. Complete the job, and the money is released to your account. No chasing invoices.

3

Build your reputation

Earn endorsements from real clients and grow a base of people who request you by name.
