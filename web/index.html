HOW IT WORKS

Two sides. One transaction.

Every job has a customer who needs work done and a worker who does it. The platform connects them, holds the payment, and releases it when the work is confirmed.

CUSTOMER SIDE

1

Post a job

Describe what you need, set your budget, and publish. Workers in your area see it immediately.

2

Review and hire

Workers apply with their price and message. Pick the one you trust, and your payment is held securely.

3

Confirm and pay

When the work is done, confirm completion. The worker gets paid. You can endorse their work.

WORKER SIDE

1

Find work

Browse the Live Market for jobs that match your skills, or publish your ID Card and let customers come to you.

2

Do the work, get paid

Your payment is secured before you start. Complete the job, and the money is released to your account. No chasing invoices.

THE PROMISE

Built for trust

Every design decision is tested against one question: is this fair to the worker and clear to the customer?

10% flat fee, always visible

No hidden charges. The platform fee is shown on every receipt as a line item. You always know what the worker received.

Payment held before work begins

When you hire, your payment is secured immediately. The worker knows they'll be paid before they show up. That's worker dignity.

Endorse, don't rate

No star ratings. When the work is done, you endorse it or raise a concern. Binary, honest, and dignified.

FOR EVERYONE

Every user is both customer and worker

There's no role selection at sign-up. No permanent labels. You post a job when you need help, and you apply for work when you have time. The same person can do both in the same week. XProHub is built for people who show up and do the work — and sometimes need work done themselves.
